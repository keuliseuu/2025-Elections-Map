2025 National Election Interactive Map

    Senatorial Rankings
    Data aggregated from the Commission on Elections (COMELEC). Interactive map powered by Leaflet. Developed for educational and informational purposes.
    Important Notes
    Data Coverage

    Some locations may not display results. This is due to pending submissions of election results. As of May 16, 799 precincts have yet to return their election results to COMELEC.

    Limitations

    This interactive map only includes results for the Senatorial race and does not include Overseas Absentee Voting (OAV) counts
